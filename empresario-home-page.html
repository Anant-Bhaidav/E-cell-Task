About Empresario

Empresario is the annual business model competition organized by Entrepreneurship Cell, IIT Kharagpur. Entrepreneurship Cell is searching for innovative and enterprising ideas through Empresario. Empresario'25 provides a platform for budding entrepreneurs to get excellent professional mentorship from our experienced, varied, and distinguished mentor pool, and to showcase their ideas in front of Venture Capitalists, Angel Investors, and Industry Leaders.

Tracks

Product and Services Track

A Business Model that is function oriented and is aimed at providing sustainability of both consumption and production. Ideas/Prototypes based on meeting diverse consumer requirements or providing an innovative service.

Social Track

A Business Model that focuses on adding value to the people at the bottom of the pyramid and/or help building a greener tomorrow. Empresario's very own track for enterprises that have a positive social or environmental impact.

IIT KGP Track

Are you a student of IIT KGP? Got some high-potential ideas? This track is exclusively for innovative Business Models of IIT Kharagpur students. The majority of the team members ( 50% or more) should be from IIT Kharagpur.

AI/ML Track

A business model that is intelligence-oriented and is aimed at enabling innovation, efficiency, and scalability through Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning. Ideas/Prototypes based on solving diverse real-world challenges, enhancing decision-making, or providing intelligent, automated, and personalized services to meet evolving user needs.

Initiatives & Events

Expert Mentoring

Semi-finalists receive guidance from seasoned entrepreneurs and industry professionals including top VC firms like Blume Ventures and Eximius Ventures to refine their ideas and strategies.

Networking

An excellent opportunity to connect with entrepreneurs, innovative thinkers, potential partners, and investors from world-class firms like Venture Catalysts and Elevation Capital.

Startup Services

To enhance growth, startups gain premium software access and credits on leading platforms like AWS, CRM services, or Incubation support.

Elevator Pitch

Participants pitch their ventures to top angel investors, VCs, and industry leaders from renowned firms like JP Morgan Chase & Co. and Rolls-Royce. This is a unique chance to showcase innovation and attract potential investment.

Prize Money

Awards will be given to winners and runners-up in Products & Services, Social Impact, and Best IIT Kharagpur Entry from a prize pool of over ₹1.2 crore. This investment highlights our commitment to supporting innovative ideas.

Feedback

Valuable, actionable feedback on business models from our expert judging panel of entrepreneurs and investors, empowering you to sharpen your approach and enhance your venture's success.
